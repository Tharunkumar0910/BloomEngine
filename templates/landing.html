Bloom's Taxonomy

Six Cognitive Levels

Benjamin Bloom's framework organizes cognitive learning into six progressive levels, from basic recall to complex creation.

1
Remember
Recall facts and basic concepts
2
Understand
Explain ideas in own words
3
Apply
Use information in new situations
4
Analyze
Draw connections and examine
5
Evaluate
Justify decisions and positions
6
Create
Produce original work and designs
How It Works

Four Simple Steps

From uploading your questions to exporting classified results — BloomStudio handles the entire workflow automatically.

Upload Questions

2
